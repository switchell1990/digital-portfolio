Hello, I am Angel Witchell and I have created this portfolio to show off my work to future employers and students. I am currently studying Computing level 2 that contains creative IT and System Support. The work I will showcase in this portfolio is Graphics design, Animations, Web design, and Unit 1 Online Worlds. I will be explaining in depth about each unit and if I enjoyed them or if I disliked them and why? I will also be explaining how I felt about the units when I first starting to study them and if I found them hard or easy to complete. To explore what units I have done goto the unit page where can be found on the buttons or if you are using mobile or a tablet goto the top right hand corner and click the three white lines and you will find another navigation linking to the home page, about page, units page, and contact page.

In this portfolio when you want to view a piece of my work on the unit page, you will not need to download my work because it will be displayed as a button that will link you to a PDF file, that requires no download. I have done this way because I know employers can be in a rush or simply do not do have the time to download work because there can be issues with download speeds or download links that can stop working but with a PDF file, you find that it will always be on the internet.
